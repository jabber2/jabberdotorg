On Thursday 19 October 2006 12:06, WL Gardner wrote:
> I should not need to "subscribe" to myself in order to "probe" or get my
> presence, right?
>
> I want to do the opposite of this:
>
> <presence from="will at jabber.org" to="will@ jabber.org ">
>     <show>chat</show>
> </presence>
>
> I want to get my status instead of setting it.

You get this automatically from all other resources without having to 
subscribe to yourself.  (e.g. log onto a server with two Psis.)

That being said, if you attempt to do this from a dead connection it's not 
going to work.  So the best way to determine if your existing connection is 
online is probably to ping yourself. :-)
