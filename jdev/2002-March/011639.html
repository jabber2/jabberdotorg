On Sun, 10 Mar 2002, Amarnath Yara wrote:

> But I had problems with the logins which had capital letters in the login
> names. For the users whose login names have capital letters the transport
> login is failing for yahoo, msn etc.

See
http://mailman.jabber.org/pipermail/jdev/2002-February/010146.html

Yes, there is a bug in most transports concerning this. CVS AIM-t at least
indicates in its error message that failed transport logins may be due to
case sensitivity. Fixing is a bit difficult because of downwards
compatibility but should be possible.

Hash is computed this way:
echo -n "jid(case sensitive)" | sha1sum
