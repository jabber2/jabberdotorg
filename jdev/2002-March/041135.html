Hi Jose!

This is just a suggestion, but unless:

a) you're going to create a closed-source client,
b) it is not feasible to implement in an existing client what you consider to be a "must-have" feature, or
c) you just feel the need for absolute control over the project...

...please consider contributing to one of the existing client projects before beginning your own. High-quality clients are in demand; duplicated efforts tend to antagonize production thereof.
