I am not too sure what level of complexity you are looking to go for, but <plug type='shameless'>JabminRPC (https://www.jabberstudio.org/projects/view.php?id=10)  could be used to do this very thing. </plug> Of course it's not java, but as long as your client speaks JabberRPC(Jabber flavoured XMLRPC) its not a problem at all to interface to add/remove/change users stuff without directly manipulating the user.xml file (We let jabberd handle all of that). Plus there are other nifty features and of course we are working on more. So check it out. See if that can help.
