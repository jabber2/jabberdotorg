This is just a reminder that the Jabber Software Foundation is currently 
accepting applications to join the organization. The main responsibility 
of JSF members is to choose the Jabber Council and the Board of 
Directors. In addition, you must be a JSF member to serve on the Jabber 
Council. This year you can apply simply by creating a page on the wiki:

http://wiki.jabber.org/index.php/MembershipApplications

I will lock down that page at the close of business on July 15 (this 
Friday) so that voting can begin, so please fill out your application by 
then.

You will need a wiki account to apply. Unfortunately, the wiki spam has 
started, so new wiki accounts are being created only on request from one 
of the Sysops (e.g., me):

http://wiki.jabber.org/index.php/Sysops

Thanks!
