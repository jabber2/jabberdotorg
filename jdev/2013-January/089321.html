and video but use XMPP for messaging and presence. Emil Ivov, Enrico
Marocco, and I have written an Internet-Draft containing
recommendations for implementing and deploying such clients. If you've
developed such a dual-stack client, we'd love to receive your
feedback! The spec is here:

http://tools.ietf.org/html/draft-ivov-xmpp-cusax-02
