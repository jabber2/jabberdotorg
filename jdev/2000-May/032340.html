I just wanted to keep everyone in the loop:

For those that didn't notice, the 1.0 server release was uploaded Monday
to: http://download.jabber.org/dists/1.0/  I'll be writing an announcement
for it and sending that out hopefully tonight.

Also, we have a brand new jabber.org website, thanks to mod_virgule by
raph, which is what advogato.org is based on.  It's live but still in
"test" mode, so check it out and help get its integration smoothed out in
the next few days.  We appreciate any/all feedback to the
web-dev at jabber.org mailing list, join if you're interested in helping!

For those that don't know, you'll see a link from the new jabber.org to
the jabber.com and jabbercentral.com sites, looks for some announcements
about these in the *very* near future :)

One last thing, I'm going to be writing a "Project Status Update" on the
project as a whole within the next two weeks, and we'll have as many
components (transports, clients, libraries) released by that time.  The
intention is to make Jabber a little more presentable and bring everything
in sync.

Thanks again to everyone who's helped in some way, it's been a busy few
months!

Jer
