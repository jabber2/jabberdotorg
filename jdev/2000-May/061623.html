What you want to do is log into the CVS server as follows:

	set CVSROOT=:pserver:anoncvs at jabber.org:/home/cvs
	cvs login

When prompted for a password, enter "anoncvs".  Then do:

	cvs -z3 co java-webb

That'll get you all the files.
