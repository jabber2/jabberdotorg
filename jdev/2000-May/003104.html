Sean Higgins wrote:

> I have gotten a jabber server up and running fairly quickly and it
> seems to work well, but I am looking to add several transports
> including aim, icq, and yahoo.  I am missing something very basic,
> but I can not seem to find any documentation about it.  I have gotten
> the transport code, uncompress it, and go to run ./configure, but
> there is no such file.  It appears that I need to put the transport
> as a subdirectory of something else that already has a ./configure,
> but I am not certain where.

aim, icq, yahoo, msn and irc-transports are not official released yet.

You need automake and autoconf to install these transports.
Execute ./autogen.sh, it creates the configure-skript. After
that, its the normal ./configure , make , make install routine.
