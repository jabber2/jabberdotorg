Hello,

collecting and building DEBs for Jabber on
http://sites.inka.de/lina/debian/. So just put the following lines in your
/etc/apt/sources.list file:

deb http://sites.inka.de/lina/debian i386/
deb http://sites.inka.de/lina/debian all/

and do "apt-get -ufm update ; apt-get install gabber libadns0" to get the
DEBs.
