since the upgrade to Drupal almost a month ago. We've just fixed that,

http://www.jabber.org/servers -- we've had to change the name of
servers.xml -- I've changed it to be basicservers.xml (there is a
permanent HTTP redirect in place). It is basicservers because we may

components hosted at the servers etc.).
