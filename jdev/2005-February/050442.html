I've posted an update about the JabberStudio service here:

http://www.jabberstudio.org/

Also, we are currently having a bit of trouble with Samba mounts between
our new server machines, so websites for validated JabberStudio projects 
such as http://exodus.jabberstudio.org/ are returning a 404 error. We're
working to get that fixed as soon as possible.

Please note that I am not the owner or proprietor of JabberStudio, which
is hosted on JSF machines but is independently owned and operated by two 
longtime Jabber developers, Thomas Muldowney and Ryan Eatmon (they both 
got involved many months before I did). While I am happy to help with
some admininstration issues such as granting ssh access to the dev box,
Thomas and Ryan have their own schedule for reinstating full JabberStudio 
service (bug reporting, etc.), and Thomas is also the contact point for
project developers who have validated that their code is clean. Please
see the earlier announcement for information about project validation:

http://www.jabberstudio.org/recover/

My apologies for the continued inconvenience. The admins are no happier
about any of this than you are.
