Some time ago, we moved old documents to:

http://www.jabber.org/old-docs/

However, documents that were hopelessly out of date were not moved
(IIRC, we set up forwards to appropriate newer locations). For instance,
the "Jabber Protocol Overview" is pretty much superseded by the XMPP
RFCs and various JEPs -- see http://www.jabber.org/protocol/ -- and the
"Jabber Programmer's Guide" was really not a programming guide at all,
merely an overview of all the (older) protocols, which has effectively
been superseded by the RFCs and JEPs as well. Feel free to refer to the
JPG (etc.) but don't take them as canonical documentation.
