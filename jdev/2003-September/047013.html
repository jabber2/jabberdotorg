The Internet Engineering Steering Group (IESG) has issued a Last Call 
for comments on the two main XMPP Internet Drafts:

http://www.ietf.org/mail-archive/ietf-announce/Current/msg26218.html
http://www.ietf.org/mail-archive/ietf-announce/Current/msg26250.html

This is your last chance to provide comments on these documents before
the IESG votes to consider them as Proposed Standards.

Your thorough review of these documents is greatly appreciated before 
the end of the comment period (October 8 for "XMPP IM", October 9 for 
"XMPP Core"). Please send editorial nits directly to the document editor
(i.e., me: <stpeter at jabber.org> via email or Jabber). Please send more
substantive comments to the IESG (<iesg at ietf.org>) and cc the mailing
list of the XMPP Working Group (<xmppwg at jabber.org>).

Thank you for your attention.

Peter
