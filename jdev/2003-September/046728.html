Internet2 Chooses Jabber/XMPP

Internet2, a consortium of over 200 universities that is working with
government and industry to define the next-generation Internet, has
formed an instant messaging working group that will standardize on
Jabber/XMPP technology for campus-wide and inter-campus IM networks.
