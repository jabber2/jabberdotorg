Just to add to Rob's remarks - though the only immediate use for this
will be in transports, this can be a much more widely useful tool.

Examples include: you might want your Internet dating service to add and
remove prospective partners, your significant other to look at your
roster, or the office bot to subscribe you to potential customers.

Permission management is, of course, the problem here.  Simple cases
like transports can probably be handled without client support, but more
complicated cases definitely can't be done on the server alone.
