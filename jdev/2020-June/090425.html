During the next (virtual) Berlin XMPP Meetup on Wednesday, June 10th,
18:00 CEST, Daniel Gultsch and me will give a short introduction to the
technologies used for audio/video calls with XMPP by example of the A/V
support recently added to Conversations.  Topics discussed will include
Jingle, ICE/STUN/TURN, (S)RTP, and WebRTC.  As time permits, we'll also
hear an interesting bonus talk by eta on integrating the A/V solution
with Asterisk.

The meetup will be held on Jitsi Meet, and we'll also be streaming live
to YouTube.  See:

	https://0x0.st/Hxz3

If you have any questions or comments, please join our MUC room:
