The only transports I know of that have been specifically rewritten as jabberd loadable modules are groupchat (in the "conference" module) irc-transport and irc-gateway (in the "irc-jabber" module).

to grab the modules from cvs:

$ cvs -d :pserver:anoncvs at jabber.org:/home/cvs login
password: anoncvs

$ cvs -d :pserver:anoncvs at jabber.org:/home/cvs co <module_name>
