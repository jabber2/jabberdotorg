This is XML-escaping, and is standart in XML. You should always escape chars
'"', "'", '<', '>', and '&'.

Here's a table of escaped chars. Also, always escape those chars, otherwise
you may send bad-formatted XML.

    & -> &amp;
    < -> &lt;
    > -> &gt;
    " -> &quot;
    ' -> &apos;
