Hello,

thanks for telling me about bugs and stuff. Since many of you
have been discussing this 'group in groups' feature, I decided to add it
into
the client to see what it looked like. I used the file-ish "/"-method, i e
"group1/subgroup1". This is semi-compatible with Winjab (Winjab shows flat
group hierarchy - group1 will be sibling with group1/subgroup1).

I also put in the autocomplete feature when you specify whom the message
will be sent to (nice feature of Outlook). There is also a possibility to
report bugs and send feedback through the help-menu.

By the way, it is built in Delphi 5 (someone asked) and screenshots are
available at Hotjabber.com (small camera icon).

Bjorn
