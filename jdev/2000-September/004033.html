Amar,

  I think you are thinking about groupchat the wrong way.. groupchat is more like joining an IRC channel, or a Yahoo Conference... you join the group friends on conference.jabber.org, and anyone else in that group will show up, and you can talk with them in that group... it is currently not a "mailing list" type of transport, if it were, then they would be public.. i.e. if you sent to friends at conference.jabber.org, and another person did too, they would go to the same group of people...
