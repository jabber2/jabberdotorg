Yes, BlockedList is the active list of the session. I sent the fallowing
stanza
to set the active list ( RFC section 10. 4) before sending messages to block
the user.

SEND:
-----------------------------------
<iq type="set" id="141902667241"> <query xmlns="jabber:iq:privacy"> <active
name="BlockedList"></active>
</query>
</iq>

-----------------------------------

RECV [59]:
------------------------------------
'<iq xmlns='jabber:client' id='141902667241' type='result'/>'
------------------------------------
