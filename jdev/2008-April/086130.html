> I am currently experimenting with IPv6 and I am curious to know whether

Our Jabber server (jabberd14 code base) has been running on our IPv6
network for over 4 years without any problems.  We use IPv6 on the
client side (c2s), we're also running tranports/services that are
connected to the main service over IPv6 and we establish quite some s2s
connections over IPv6 too.

Next to Jabber/XMPP, I'm already using so much services over IPv6 that
if IPv4 drops dead (DHCP issues), I don't even notice until I try to
browse the web.
