Dear FreeSWITCHers,

	Cluecon, a 3-Day Telephony User and Developer Conference, will have
its 4th Annual Conference on August 4 to 7, 2008, targeting
telecommunication CEOs, entrepreneurs, software programmers and VOIP
enthusiasts.

I'm calling on everyone that is interested in speaking email me ASAP.
If you're interested in Sponsoring Cluecon please email me as well.

The conference will be staged at Hilton Garden Hotel, downtown
Chicago.  The event will be promoted via press release and our partner
websites.  This opportunity will enable your company to talk about
your innovations and the technologies you use in your
implementations.  This is also an opportune time to create contacts in
the VOIP / PBX world and to gain potential customers.

Each speaker will have to choose his own topic but would have to
coordinate with me on the schedules and content.  Their might be a
speaker who's already chose the topic you have chosen.

I look forward to seeing you on August 4, 2007 in Chicago.
