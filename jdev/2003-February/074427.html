Has a anyone looked at writing or written a Jabber<=>Jabber
gateway/transport?

I know this sounds a bit stupid but it would actually be useful for people
with multiple accounts, as it would provide some level of JID aliasing that
I think is very lacking in the Jabber spec.

eg when I am at work, I sign in to me at work.com/work and subscribe to the J2J
transport that logs me into me at home.name/home - now my co-workers and
customers can see me online as me at work, and my friends see me online as
me at home.name, but I only need to run one Jabber client and it doesn't need
to be modified in anyway to handle multiple accounts.

If there is a better way to do this, can someone please let me know or point
me in the right direction...
