If you want to access Jabber from .Net, you could try out Jabber-Net, which
is a native .Net library, instead of using COM interop.  Put it in your
toolbox, drop a JabberClient on your design surface, fill out the connection
properties, double-click on the OnMessage event, etc.
 
http://www.jabberstudio.org/projects/jabber-net/project/view.php
<http://www.jabberstudio.org/projects/jabber-net/project/view.php>
