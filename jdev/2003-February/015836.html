There is a rate limiting mechanism called karma within the jabber.xml.  This
needs to be adjusted to increase the amount of bandwidth allowed through.
The problem is, images that are too large will cause your session to wait
until you finish recieving that sent image which could cause problems.  Its
probably a good idea to keep it limited to small images.
