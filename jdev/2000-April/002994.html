I got this before too on Solaris 2.5.1 and fixed it...  now what did I
do...

Well, I can't seem to find it now, but the problem was that libxode was
compiled with the profiling switch turned on.  Once I took that out in
the libxode makefile and recompiled it the other parts worked 100%.

Oh wait!  I just found it.  It's the -pg switch.  It causes the
profiling code to be used.  Just turn that off in the libxode
src/Makefile.

Hope that helps.
