Duane -

To remove a Roster item, send an iq like:
<iq type="set"><query xmlns="jabber:iq:roster"><item jid="foo at jabber.org"
subscription="remove"/></query></iq>

This should remove the item.
You will often get 'set' type iq's with a jabber:iq:roster namspace attribute.
These are "Roster Pushes", that get sent to you after you change, or add a
roster item.
