  As a quick description, a 'resource' is simply a desigantor for 'forms of
self'.  A quick example might be myself, with my resources being perhaps:

  Home
  Work
  Pager
  Jabba

  ANY of the above is fine.  Really, there is no formatting for a 'Resource'
needed, basically, any normal text you would wish.  There may be a character
limitation, but I'm unsure of what it is at the moment..
