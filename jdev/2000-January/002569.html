First, goto http://mailman.jabber.org/listinfo , find the lists you're in and
add your new e-mail like you're newly joining these lists. Then, after you
replied to the 'list-authenciation' message (coming to your new mail address),
go and delete your old addresses in the list. So that you won't lose any mails
sent between the time you delete your old mail, and the time you add your new
mail.

  I suggest you to get a free forwarding address from http://www.iname.com ,
or any other mail service, and join to the lists by that mail address... Then,
the only thing you will have to do will be, changing where that
free-forwarding-mail forwards incoming mails.
