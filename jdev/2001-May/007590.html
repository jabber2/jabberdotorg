Take a look at xdb_java, as John Hebert suggests.  You should be able to add
your own modules to it to talk to the App Server for the services you
mention.  (Or, at least that's what Matt told me the last time we met :) )

It is a budding project, but is constantly being improved and we plan to use
it on a large scale here, so be assured it is not intended to be a toy
project.
