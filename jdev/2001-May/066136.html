I have to agree with temas's description of a resource. Location won't really convey
all the possible future uses of where a jabber enabled client will be used. Remember
that jabber is not necessarily a chat protocol.

I can easily imagine simultaneously running a number of jabber-enabled clients such
as an RSS viewer/agent, a jabber enabled multiplayer game, and a chat client.
Depending on one client to provide all of those services is wishful thinking, but
not realistic.
