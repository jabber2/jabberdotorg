The folks at PocketLinux had success in porting Jabber to their platform
(see http://www.pocketlinux.com/ for info) and I know there was at least
one Jabber client under development for PalmOS and one for WinCE
(http://jabber.org/?oid=288), but I'm not sure about other platforms. I
notice that there will be a session on "Jabber & Embedded Devices" at
JabberCon 2001 (http://www.jabbercon.com/), maybe some good initiatives
will come out of that. :)
