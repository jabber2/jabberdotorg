On 16 May 2001 16:05:45 -0400, Mike Szczerban wrote:
> Hi all,
> 
> last night I release version 0.6.0 of the JabberTools Importer -- its
> first public release.  Essentially, it allows one to import contacts and
> buddies from different IM programs -- AOL Instant Messenger, GnomeICU,
> Gaim, mICQ, and Licq are all stable in implementation, with win32 ICQ
> support forthcoming.  
> 
> The importer runs both in command-line and web-based interfaces, is
> written in Perl, and uses Net::Jabber.
> 
> The JabberTools project website is http://jabbertools.sourceforge.net
> and a tarball of the package is available at
> http://sourceforge.net/project/showfiles.php?group_id=20248&release_id=35297 .
> 
> Justin Mecham's http://www.jabbertools.org site also has a working
> implementation of the web-based program.
> 
> Try it out; let me know how you fare.  Also, if you have any experience
> dealing with win32 ICQ databases, I need help with that.
> 
> Feature requests and comments are, of course, more than welcome.
> 
> 
> Thanks,
> Mike Szczerban
