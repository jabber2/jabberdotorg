For all those who are interested in joining the Jabber Foundation (see 
http://foundation.jabber.org/ for more information), I wanted you to 
know that in yesterday's online meeting we decided that the open 
membership form will be up for one more week, i.e., through the end of 
the day 2001-05-23 (I suppose we'll stipulate 24:00 UTC as the time 
it'll be taken down, if there is such a thing as 24:00 UTC :P). If you 
have any questions, concerns, or problems with the form (the earlier IE 
problem has been fixed), please let me know.
