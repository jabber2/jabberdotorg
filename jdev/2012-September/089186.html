As Kevin pointed out a <message> stanza with type of remove would not be
valid, as that was what you originally said was being sent.

That trace however shows an <iq> stanza with type of set and sub elements
to remove an item from a roster (which is not what you originally said was
being sent). Which is why there was some confusion, you need to be accurate
about what is being sent :-)

It looks like your client is specifically requesting removal of the item
from the roster, rather than just cancelling a subscription for presence.

If I remember correctly in pidgin you can right click a user in the roster
and cancel presence subscription rather than removing the person from your
"buddy list".

Cheers

Kirk
