> Search in the XEP list for 'private xml storage', that protocol
> allows you to store arbitrary (by XMPP restricted) XML on the
> server. And most servers support that protocol these days.
>
> This would be the URL you are looking for:
>
>    http://www.xmpp.org/extensions/xep-0049.html
>
> Unfortunately www.xmpp.org doesn't resolve for me yet, and you
