Abu, we discussed right here how to get the x.o/n website working.

Linux User:
open /etc/hosts
Windows User:
open {WindowsFolder}\System32\drivers\etc\hosts

Add the following line:
208.245.212.99 xmpp.org www.xmpp.org blog.xmpp.org

You may need to restart your network connection for it to take effect.
