> i would like to test this 
> feature of jabber. And do you know what support jabber offer about geopriv 
> ietf wg requirementss?

Geolocation is supported via JEP-0080:

http://www.jabber.org/jeps/jep-0080.html

The IETF's geopriv WG has a complex set of requirements for permissions
and access control, which is not supported as part of JEP-0080, but any
publisher can control who has acceess to geolocation information using
the built-in subscriber controls in JEP-0060.
