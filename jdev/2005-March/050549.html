I have created a patch for the jabber yahoo! transport.

It's available at http://www.hawkesnest.net/~hawke/yahoo-t.diff.bz2

It corrects some problems I had noticed with presence for my Yahoo!
contacts.  It also maps yahoo presence as follows:
available -> online
brb, not at desk, out to lunch -> away
not at home, not in office, on vacation, stepped out -> xa
busy, on phone -> dnd

The only one I'm unsure of is "stepped out", perhaps it should map to
"away"?

This patch also maps the yahoo! "busy" flag for custom presence to
"away".  Perhaps this should be "dnd"?

and finally, it maps the yahoo automatic "idle" message to x/a.  I'm not
sure if this is the best way to do it.

Suggestions are welcome.
