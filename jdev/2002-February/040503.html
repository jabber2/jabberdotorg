You can register internationalized test domains at networksolutions. But
it's nearly sure that they are encoded with an early draft of IDN that
will not be used when this sort of domains go into production.
Also they can only be used under .mltbd.com/.net/.org yet ;)

If somebody is interested in the work of the IDN working group at the
IETF see http://www.ietf.org/html.charters/idn-charter.html
