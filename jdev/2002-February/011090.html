> Where is the S2S protocol documented?
> The only thing I could find was the dialback document.

That's basically it.  You use dialback to authenticate the S2S then use the
resulting connection as sort of a multiplexed client-server connection where
the sending server is acting on behalf of all of its users.  The same
protocols as the jabber:client protocols apply except they're in the
jabber:server namespace.
