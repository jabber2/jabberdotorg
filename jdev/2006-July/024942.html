Jabber.org support old jabber-style authentication feature, hence the  
reason why you can login with the old authentication methods.

XMPP servers normally does not support any authentication mechanisms  
that is considered insecure (ie. old jabber-style authentication and  
plain-text authentication) unless TLS is at least secured.  In this  
case, XMPP servers will normally not broadcast the types of  
authentication methods it supports until you negotiate a TLS  
connection.  Afterwards, during the second handshake, it will provide  
you with a list of authentication mechanisms it can support.
