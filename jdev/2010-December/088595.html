Morning folks,

The XSF Interop Week is getting off to a slow (and, in this part of  
the world, frosty) start - we're gradually getting into the chatroom  
xmpp:interop at muc.xmpp.org?join - that's interop at muc.xmpp.org for  
those without XMPP IRI parsers.

Of course, if you can't join that, we already have an interop  
problem, of course, but it's known to be reachable from jabber.org  
accounts if needs be.

There is also a mailing list, interop at xmpp.org, which you can join by  
sending email to interop-request at xmpp.org (or via the new fangled web  
thing at http://mail.jabber.org/mailman/listinfo/interop of course).

I'll be spinning up the Isode servers across today, with an Isode hat  
on (mostly).
