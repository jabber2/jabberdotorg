Hi,

FrOSCon is a yearly two-day conference at the Bonn-Rhein-Sieg University of Applied Sciences near Cologne, Germany.
After some initial positive feedback from the community, I stepped forward and applied for a developer room at this years FrOSCon, which will happen 20.-21. August. This allows us to share knowledge and discuss ideas with other XMPP developers, enthusiasts and interested conference participants - things we typically did at XSF Summit or FOSDEM which didn't happen for two years due to Covid. We can talk about recent client or server developments, ideas for new standard extensions or discuss hot topics in instant messaging.
If you are interested in giving a talk, want to discuss a specific topic or just want to signal that you'd like to participate, please add yourself to the wiki page https://wiki.xmpp.org/web/FrOSCon_2022. If you have further questions, just reply here or send them to me directly (via xmpp:jabber at larma.de).
Hoping to finally see some of you in real life again :)
Marvin
