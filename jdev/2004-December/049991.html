The JabberStudio website (co-hosted with jabber.org) is beginning to 
consume a *lot* of bandwidth, mainly because of the software downloads.
Therefore we have begun to explore the possibility of mirroring at least
jabberstudio.org and possibly jabber.org as well. I know I have received
offers to mirror these sites in the past, but did not follow up because
the need was not urgent. Well, it is becoming urgent now. So if you work
for a company that might be willing and able to mirror these ever more
popular websites, please contact me via Jabber or (if you must) email.

Thanks!
