Hey,

I'm pleased to announce that <https://badxmpp.eu> now works with clients
too, having adjusted the configuration and added the appropriate SRV
records.

Testing a client (or -library) implementation could be done by
attempting to connect to a dummy account on the chosen test host.
Getting as far as the SASL step would mean the test has completed. Since
there are no accounts on these hosts, any attempt to authenticate will
fail. Depending on each test, this may be a good or a bad outcome, e.g.
you successfully reaching SASL at xmpp:expired.badxmpp.eu would be bad,
while reaching it on the various ipv4/ipv6 tests would be good.

Feedback, comments, suggestions welcome.
