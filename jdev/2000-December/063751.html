> To connect I have to give the complete hostname, at jabber.com
> and jabber.org you only have to give the domain.  How is this done,
> do I need to configure some record in my nameservers?

Yes.  If you have your server at jabber.oracom.com, then you need to
change the record for oracom.com to point to that machine as well.  I
checked with nslookup and oracom.com does not resolve to anything
currently.
