BTW, if you think XHTML messages increase bandwidth usage, just wait
till you see the packet sizes once Jabber clients start sending message
and presence info using end-to-end PGP/GPG. Peter Millard has PGP
support going in a development version of WinJab, and here's a rough
estimate of the message size for saying "hi" ...

[stpeter at squire /tmp]$ ls -l hi*
-rw-rw-r--    1 stpeter  stpeter      1191 Dec 12 11:05 hi-pgp.txt
-rw-rw-r--    1 stpeter  stpeter        99 Dec 12 11:07 hi-plain.txt
