horrible, horrible idea.

These are two separate servers, and the roster and other user data will most
definately reflect this.

In other words - untold horrors will happen if you attempt to use multiple
virtual domains, and still treat the result as a single domain. If nothing
else, most clients will not show you in their rosters when you log into a
different server than the one you added them to your roster on. Remote hosts
certainly don't have the logic to equate these two machine names as the
same.

Of course, that smiley may have been an indicator that it is all a joke..
but man I don't want people to start trying this ;-)

-David Waite
