Hello,

You need to add the <msntrans/> section.

<service id="msn.domain">
  <load><msntrans>./jabber2/msn-transport/msntrans.so</msntrans></load>

  <msntrans xmlns="jabber:msntrans:config">
    <instructions>Enter your MSN Messenger account and password. Example: user1 at hotmail.com.</instructions>
  </msntrans>
</service>
