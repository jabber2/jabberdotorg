You can of course run virtualhosts, but you actually want separate
servers.
The only way to really get two functional servers out of the deal is to
have multiple IPs on the box (not hard to set up) and have them listen on
different IPs. Otherwise, one will listen on 5269 and 5222, blocking the
other server from working.
