Yes, jabber supports this, just add multple <group> tags..
i.e.
<iq type='set'><query xmlns='jabber:iq:roster'><item jid='foo at bar.org'
name='my user'...><group>group1</group><group>group2</group></query></iq>

and voila! ;)
