Currently, DNSSEC is not supported for .IM-based domains because of lack
of support on the side of .IM TLD operator.
In reply to my request, the operator has expressed some interest, and
asked other interested domain owners to email them to special mailbox
dnssec at nic.im . The more public interest, the more priority this feature
gets on their development timeline.
So please drop them a line to express your interest.
Please share this info with other .IM domains owners.
Reposting this info in other related maillists is appreciated.
