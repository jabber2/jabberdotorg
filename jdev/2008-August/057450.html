Currently a node cannot be in two collections. PSA was going to  
seperate out the collection portion of the spec to simplify xep-60 and  
as collections are less mature. I would reference leaf nodes within a  
leaf with some sort of node-linking ns in the meantime to get a  
similar effect instead of using collections.
