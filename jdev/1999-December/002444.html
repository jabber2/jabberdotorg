The Simple Markup Language, SML, is an attempt to reduce
XML to the bare bones. We have a mailing list and an
active group of developers working to establish a common
usage subset of XML. Input from someone on the Jabber Development
Team, especially defining use cases and implications for Jabber
of some of our choices would be very useful. I hope that someone
of the Jabber developers will at least lurk the SML-DEV list
and holler if we dfo anything that seems really stupid. Even
better would be active participation. 

We are not trying to steal Jabber developers <g> but there
could be impacts here and we really could use your input. 

For info: 
http://www.egroups.com/group/sml-dev/info.html
