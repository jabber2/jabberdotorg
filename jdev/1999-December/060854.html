I am also thankful for the Jabber project.  I'm just frustrated that a
simple query about the status of the Jabber.dll turned into an opportunity
for someone to bash me.

Unfortunately, the only way I can help is to try and integrate Jabber into a
ActiveX chat control I'm developing; and the only way I can do with my
current knowledge is with the .dll that has been discussed on and off over
the past 4-6 months.  Right now I have it working using UDP and I'm working
with a great codec for voice chat...can't wait to try out some stuff with

was asking about in my most recent post.

because then that person (me) has to at least make clear that he was just
asking a question.

Some people just can't wait to grab at an opportunity to make a comment that
makes them look good and someone else look bad....to me that's downright
insincere.
