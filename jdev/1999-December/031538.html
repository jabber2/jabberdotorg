  Hello all,

  I've been following Jabber for a few weeks, and just quit my "real" job to work on my startup company's new animated Jabber client (Win32).  This client will be completed by year's end.  Here's some info on it that I just sent to info at jabber.org :

  Currenly named "Jabber Toons", our client allows IM
  users to create an animated representation of
  themselves by choosing different cartoon body parts
  and colors.  The character can then walk around and
  interact with other characters in an IM session. 
  Current animations include walking, shaking hands,
  high-5's, hugging, kissing and waving.  This animation
  is combined with a standard IM text box for
  conversation.

  "Jabber Toons" will eventually be integrated into our
  companion product, IRC Toons (TM), which is scheduled
  for release next week.  IRC Toons brings IRC channels
  to life with fully animated characters.

  Lake Clear Interactive will contribute its socket
  communication and XML parsing code to the Jabber
  project (written in Delphi).   If any client authors
  would like to add our animation technology to their
  clients, please contact us ASAP.
  ....
   
  My startup partners and I look forward to working with you all and contributing to Jabber's success.
