I'm new to this list, but I'd like to make a few suggestions:

  Although this new design is aesthetically more pleasing, the navigation
and arrangement of the information is still very confusing.

  The biggest problem is that there is no way to tell what "area" (users,
developers or business) of the site you're in without looking at the URL in
the address bar. This is simple to fix: just put a heading in the yellow
navigation bar, or highlight the icon of the area that you're in, at the top
of the page.

I don't want to be a complainer, so if I can help solve these problems, or
in some other way, let me know.
