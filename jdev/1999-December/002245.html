Downloaded the new 0.8 release, and took 30 minutes to compile on an
unltra 10.  Most of the time tracking down the adns configure mixup. 
Jabber-lib incorrectly identifies adns as missing much as 0.7
incorrectly identified other libs as missing.  I hardcoded the -ladns
into the Makefiles and everything compiled just fine and passed the self
checks.

I did a very cursory see if it comes up with the zabber tcl client and
had no issues.
