Hi all!

The Free and Open Software Developers Meeting (FOSDEM) will take place on
21 and 22 February 2004 in Brussels, Belgium. It is a gathering of all people
interested in the development of Free and Open Source software, targetted
at Europe.

There will be lots of interesting speakers on various subjects and much more.
See their webpage [1] for more information. The two day event is pretty
informal (which I think is good), and a nice way to meet other developers
and exchange ideas.

I will be going there with a couple of friends and I am in the process of
setting up some Jabber promotion. More on that later.

I'd like to know who of the Jabber community is planning on going to this
event, so we can meet in person. You can either reply to this e-mail
or send me a message via Jabber at ralphm at ik.nu.
