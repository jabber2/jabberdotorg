When searching the JUD on users.jabber.org with x:data forms, the
reported type for the JID field is incorrect.  The following is sent:

<reported>
  <field var='jid' type='jid' label='JID'/>
  ...

According to JEP-0004, the type should be 'jid-single' instead of
'jid'.

I have not found any specific contact information for Users-Agent, so
I post here hoping that the right person will see this.
