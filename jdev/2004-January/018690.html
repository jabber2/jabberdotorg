On Wed, Jan 21, 2004 at 04:56:04PM -0500, Katz, Dov B (IT) wrote:
> I'm trying to rewrite C2S to host it outside the jabber server for accepting
> client connections, and notifying the server for creation and destruction of
> them, etc...  Does anyone know the message exchange needed to do this/

For jabberd 1.4, see this:

  http://www.jabberstudio.org/cgi-bin/viewcvs.cgi/jabberd/jadc2s/PROTO14?view=auto

For jabberd 2.0, see these:

  http://jabberd.jabberstudio.org/dev/docs/session.shtml
  http://jabberd.jabberstudio.org/dev/docs/component.shtml

Other servers may do different things. These protocol isn't
standardised, and probably never will be, since its closely tied to the
internal architecture of the server.
