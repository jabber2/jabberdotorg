http://protocol.buddycloud.com is created by Guilherme to check both your
XMPP install and your buddycloud install.

It checks:
- DNS
- DISCO
- Connectivity
- gives feedback on how to fix things

Next steps include:
- SSL checking:
https://github.com/buddycloud/buddycloud-tests-framework/issues/13
- Checking that servers follow XEPs correctly and point out to the sysadmin
when they are using a sub-optimal XMPP servre.

Of course we'd welcome pull requests for any XEP or service that can be
remotely tested.
