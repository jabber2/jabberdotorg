Dave Cridland wrote:
> ...  there's no need to send
> XEP-0199 every minute - instead, use it when you suspect the link is 
> down. This might be because of long term inactivity, and/or because 
> you're sending (real) traffic and getting no response. (Obviously <iq/> 
> stanzas emit a response, but normally people answer <message/> stanzas 
> too.).

Right. IMHO it's a bad practice to send a ping every 60 seconds when you 
have plenty of incoming traffic (I watch the XML Console in Psi and I 
can tell you that my session is never idle for 1 second, let alone 60!). 
You're just adding noise to the signal at that point.
