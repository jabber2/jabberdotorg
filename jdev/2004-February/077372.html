FYI, at my request the Internet Assigned Numbers Authority (IANA) has
updated its port numbers registry to reflect the IANA considerations
section of the recently-approved XMPP Core Internet-Draft. Therefore
the keyword for port 5222 is now "xmpp-client" and the keyword for 
port 5269 is now "xmpp-server", updated from "jabber-client" and
"jabber-server" respectively.

http://www.iana.org/assignments/port-numbers
