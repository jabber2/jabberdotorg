This is a stanza you got from the router and that is from the session
manager. Sure this one has a from attribute and it has been added by the
session manager.

But you asked why you don't get them in _c2s_client_sx_callback. This
callback is called for stanzas, that come from the client, not from the
router. These stanzas don't have from attributes.
