Hello,

I am proud to announce the release of the Pandion 2.0 client.

Some of the most important features are:

* Kick-ass graphical user interface
* Custom avatars and emoticons
* Tabbed chatwindows
* Multi-user conferencing
* Click & run plug-in and emoticon installation
* Compliance with the XMPP-1.0 specifications, including DNS SRV 
lookups, TLS encryption and SASL authentication
* Available in many languages

For more information, screenshots, and downloads visit:
http://www.pandion.be/
