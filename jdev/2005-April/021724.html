> Are there any efforts to have jabberd2 be a clusterisable server?  That
> is, something with those nice sexy buzz words of 'redundant' and
> 'fault-tolerant' across multiple machines without a single point of
> failure, and no mucking about with multiple domains for the user?

Jabberd2 is not currently fully fault tollerant and fully clusterable but it 
can do a certain degree of clustering as you can use multiple c2s modules 
running on different machines which provides a degree of scalability, but at 
the moment it does have the limitation that the jsm module cannot be 
clustered.
