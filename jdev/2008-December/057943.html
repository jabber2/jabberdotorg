The XSF will hold its 6th XMPP Summit in Brussels on February 7-9.

The first day (Saturday) will be held at FOSDEM 2009, featuring public
talks for larger audiences and perhaps a birthday party to celebrate the
10th anniversary of Jabber technologies.

The second two days (Sunday and Monday) will be held at another location
(probably a hotel), focusing on technology discussions and hackfests
among a smaller group of core contributors.

Full details are still being worked out, but I wanted folks to at least
know about the dates and location so that you can plan ahead.
