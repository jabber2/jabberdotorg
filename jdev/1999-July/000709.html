TwOlf at jabber.jabber.org
Admin at jabber.org
#slashdot at slashnet.jabber.org

    How will these address?  Here's what I'm gathering:

Namespace is per address, aka, Jabber.org means send it to etherx at jabber.org.  You can also append a transport at the top level, Aka: TwOlf at jabber.jabber.org would specifically route it to the transport named 'Jabber' at jabber.org.  Likewise with #slashdot at slashnet.jabber.org, except to the slashnet transport at jabber.org..  Now here's the doozie..  How does it determine this.  Here's my case:
