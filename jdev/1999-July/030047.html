cvs and WinCVS works fine for me.  i was originally just using the
command-line cvs win32 port (same options as unix CVS!) but i just got
WinCVS, mostly because of your post here ...

wincvs sucks! :>  but anyhow, just specify your CVSROOT to be
:pserver:anoncvs at jabber.org:/cvs

and the auth method to be cvs's remote passwd file.  then you just do a
"cvs login" and your password is "anoncvs".

"cvs co jabber" is command-line shorthand for "cvs checkout jabber",
which should get you your own copy of the source tree.

i only used winCVS for a few minutes and couldn't figure out how to
checkout a module from a server, so just click on "command line..." and
type

"cvs checkout jabber"

to get a new tree.  you only checkout ONCE; after that, you do "cvs
update" or click the update button once you've navigated into a
directory you'd like to update.
