Ha!
Just to be clear, we obviously do S2S : you can use your own JID to
susbcribe to feeds via Superfeedr, as long as you have created a Superfeedr
account and that you have added this JID in the settings of Superfeedr.
After that you can subscribe to feeds using your very own JID (a user, a
component, or even a server jid).

Also, it's not so wacky :) Is there any client that 'speaks' XEP 060?
