We will soon relaunch the jabber.org website (powered by Drupal!).

Unfortunately this means that if your software is listed at jabber.org
right now, you will need to re-register the software so that it will be
listed at the new software pages. (But fortunately this means that we
will clear out a lot of dead projects. ;-)

I think that the Drupal setup is ready for testing, so if you want your
software project (server, client, component, etc.) to appear at the new
website, please do the following:

1. Visit http://stage.jabber.org/

2. Register an account at http://stage.jabber.org/user/register

3. Wait for one of the admins to approve your account (or poke someone
on the webteam at jabber.org list or in the webteam at conference.jabber.org
chatroom).

4. Add your software via one of the following pages:

   http://stage.jabber.org/node/add/client

   http://stage.jabber.org/node/add/component

   http://stage.jabber.org/node/add/library

   http://stage.jabber.org/node/add/server

If you find any bugs, please report them on the webteam at jabber.org list:

http://mail.jabber.org/mailman/listinfo/webteam

Thanks!

Peter
