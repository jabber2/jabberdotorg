Hi,
    Is there a way to change the password used by a transport once it's registered? In the absence of a (known) direct password change mechanism, I attempted to remove the transport association and add it back with a different password. But, even if I tried to remove the association with 'set' and </remove>, it worked as if I'd only unsubscribed from the transport - i.e. the entry in spool/yahoo.thecomputer.omnipod.com is unaffected. Further, when I attempt to just send a different password (without the <remove/> tag), I receive the same "Removing Subscription" reply, and the password is still not changed.

SENT: <iq type="set" id="wj_delreg_wj_5" to="yahoo.thecomputer.omnipod.com"><query xmlns="jabber:iq:register"><username>user1000</username><password>*****</password><remove/>
<key>6c4d1f95f3855f1df08d2de28a75fd154dacf74f</key></query></iq>

RECV: <presence to='user1000 at thecomputer.omnipod.com/Home' from='yahoo.thecomputer.omnipod.com/registered' type='unsubscribe'><status>Removing Subscription</status></presence>

Thank you for any assistance.
