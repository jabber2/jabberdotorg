I'll help you out, but first - are you trying to connect as a component into
the server's trust (so you can do things like access XDB of user accounts), or
are you trying to be a completely independant server piece? the 'service'
section in the jabber.xml points towards component access, but the use of
jabber:server implies that you are trrying to be separate (jabber:server is for
s2s communication, not for components)

-David Waite
