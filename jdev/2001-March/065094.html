Howdy Eric.

The problem is most probably that IQ packets have to be directed at a
particular session, meaning you need to specify the resource of the recipient
as well.

e.g.

<iq type='set' id='sendurl' to='comm at dev.5390.com/comm1'> ........

-David Waite
