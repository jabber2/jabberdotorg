Not sure how many folks on this list are aware of it, but Mitch Kapor and
a bunch of other smart people are creating an open-source "personal
information manager" that many folks hope could supplant MS Outlook. And
one of the core technologies for their platform is Jabber. So if you're
interested in things like Jabber-enabled calendars, integrating presence
into an email client, and building a killer app other than IM using Jabber
technology, I encourage you to get involved with this project. I've been
active on the mailing lists already but haven't seen any other Jabberites
there. Plus DJ Adams will be happy that they're using RDF. :)

I blogged about it here, lots of links to relevant pages:

http://www.saint-andre.com/blog/2002-10.html#2002-10-20T20:38

Jabber on!
