Per a vote of the Jabber Council, JEP-0016 (Server-Based Privacy Rules)
has been advanced to a status of Draft. This is a standards-track
proposal for flexible whitelisting and blacklisting (and the storage of
such lists server-side). In about 60 days I'll be asking for developers to
share their implementation experience with this protocol, so get busy. :))

The JEP is here:

http://www.jabber.org/jeps/jep-0016.html
