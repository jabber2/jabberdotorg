I would like it to be known that this is not directly a matter for the
Jabber Software Foundation, and that the JSF does not officially take a
position regarding this potential GPL violation. If there is a violation
of the GPL involved, that is a matter for the Free Software Foundation,
the code author, and the potential violator.

In addition, the JSF does not encourage or condone any misuse or abuse of
the message boards hosted by Integrity Messenger. Let the interested
parties work out their dispute without pressure or abuse. Of course you
are free to express your opinion to the parties involved, but please
understand that you do so without the sanction of the Jabber Software
Foundation.

Thank you.

Peter
