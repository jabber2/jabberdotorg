- DJB's license: you are not allowed to distribute modifications.

- Quite peculiar directory structure requiring modifaction:

- DJB's and it's followers dogmatic attitude (IMHO).

> Give it a try, you will love it.

Not really, if you want to adhere to standards conflicting with
DJB's design.
