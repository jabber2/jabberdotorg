Honestly, as cool as it would be to see official support in a JEP for

not really a protocol issue. It's simply a difference in how the socket

that - no SSL is a big one.

I did manage to get Flash into not appending the terminating slash / to
the opening <stream:stream> tag, so it's really just a matter of the
zero-byte.
