If any client developers are interested in adding support for the
experimental JEP-0127, there is a jid (lbs at movsoftware.com) that will
broadcast a sample CAP alert to a list of JID's. Currently the jid only
supports stanzas of the form:

<message from='KSTO at NWS.NOAA.GOV' 
         to='weatherbot at jabber.org'
         id='KSTO1055887203'>
  <alert xmlns='http://www.incident.com/cap/1.0'>
...
</message>

The JID will send a CAP alert to its subscribed list every 3 minutes. If you
are interested in testing out your client implementation, please send me
your JID and I will add it to the subscription list.
