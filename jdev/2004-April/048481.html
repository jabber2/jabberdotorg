Johann Romefort schrieb am 2004-04-12 18:44:15:
> A compatibility layer between Flash and Jabber2 would be the best way to 
> go IMHO.
> The thing I m wondering (due to my lack of knowledge concerning jabberd2 
> architecture),
> is what form this compatibility layer should have? Jabber Component? If 
> yes, Internal or
> External ?

There is nothing like an internal component in jabberd2. Sure you can
write an "external" component ... but you can even write it independant
from jabberd2 as a proxy ... you would just have to change the root
element and insert/delete the zero bytes.
But you will get problems with STARTTLS and SASL if you try to add
security layers.

Tot kijk
    Matthias

BTW: What you are doing with Flash is no XMPP and as far as I can see
Flash is not able to support XMPP at all. I am even not sure if I would
call it Jabber what Flash uses at present.
