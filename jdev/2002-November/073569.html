> In the meantime can can I configure mu-conference to work with spool 
> files and everything else to work with mysql?
yes, just create two xdb sections in jabber.xml file, one for xdb_file and one
for xdb_sql. then, the query dispatch will be done according to the namespaces
declared in each xdb section ...
