The old clients page at http://www.jabbercentral.org/clients/ lists a lot
of Jabber clients that are not listed on the new clients page located at
http://www.jabber.org/user/clientlist.php

This may be because some of the old clients are no longer under
development, or because some developers missed the initial call for
clients. If you use a client listed on the old page that you think should
be listed on the new page, or if you think some of the old clients could
use new developers, I would really appreciate it if you could contact the
old developers and poke them about adding their clients or handing off
their projects.

Guidelines for adding a client are here:

http://mailman.jabber.org/pipermail/jdev/2002-October/013491.html

Developers can request projects at http://www.jabberstudio.org/ (these can
be web-only if the source is proprietary or hosted on another site).

Thanks!
