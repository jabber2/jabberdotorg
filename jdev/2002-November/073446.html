If it's voice you're looking to transpit, Speex [ http://speex.org/ ] is
probabbly a better choice. It's an alternate codec, tuned more for the
purpose of voice compression than music. Speech in particular is
fundamentally simpler then music, and can be compressed to a much
smaller size while maintiaining quality, provided you know it's speech
you're working with. :)
