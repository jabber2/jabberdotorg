On 5/13/09 12:50 AM, Dieter Lunn wrote:
> I don't know if this was covered and its not on the site but what is
> the policy for usage of the xmpp logo for links to xmpp.org?

The copyright policy is here:

http://xmpp.org/about/copyright.shtml

Basically everything at the xmpp.org website is covered under the IPR
policy:

http://xmpp.org/extensions/ipr-policy.shtml

And that policy is in essence a slightly modified MIT license, which
means that there are no restrictions on use.
