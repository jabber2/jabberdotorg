It's all about security.

The ICQ transport and your Jabber server are not the same entity on the 
network, so the transport can't change (add to/remove from) your actual 
roster.  The best that could be done is for the transport to advertise 
what your "legacy" roster is using Roster Exchange[1], or it might be 
possible to apply something similar to Shared Groups[2].


-  LW

[1] JEP-0093: Roster Item Exchange 
<http://www.jabber.org/jeps/jep-0093.html>
[2] JEP-0140: Shared Groups <http://www.jabber.org/jeps/jep-0140.html>
