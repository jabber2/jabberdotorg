gloox 0.3 has just been released.
gloox is a high-level XMPP library written in C++.

This version adds JEP-0048 (Bookmark Storage), JEP-0078 (Non-SASL 
Authentication), JEP-0114 (Jabber Component Protocol), JEP-0145 
(Annotations), Roster Management and Privacy Lists.
