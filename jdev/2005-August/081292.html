I am an develper on the Google Talk Service. I have
been following this list for a year now and will continue
to do so.

I'd like to let everybody know that Google will support open
server-to-server federation, This will not happen immediately. 
We are still writing the code for server-to-server and we will 
test server-to-server with a few partners before opening it up.
See http://www.google.com/talk/developer.html for an 
updated statement on server-to-server support.

Although I will see bug reports and feature requests sent
to this mail list, it's best to enter bugs and requests at the 
Google Talk help center. We are better setup to track issues
entered through the help center. I also don't want this list to
become a Google Talk help forum. The help center
is at http://www.google.com/support/talk/bin/request.py

-- Gary
