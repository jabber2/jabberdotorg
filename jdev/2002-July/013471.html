JET is essentially a firewall piercing Jabber component that allows 
Jabber servers behind a firewall to connect to Jabber servers on the 
open Internet. JET connects through the firewall on a one way, known 
port to a known external IP address.

JET is essentially two components that are nothing more than JID 
rewriters with a socket connection between them. The internal component 
connects to the external component and passes Jabber packets back and forth.

For more information and downloads of JET, go to http://jabber.terrapin.com
