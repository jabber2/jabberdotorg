e-mail client replying to messages that are digitally signed.  For some
reason, my client, by default, encrypts outbound messages if they are
replies to messages that were digitally signed.  These messages were in
reply to rob at cataclysm.cx, who's messages are digitally signed.
 
I figured out it was doing that and realized that I had to manually
turn it off before sending.
 
I apologize for the wasted bandwidth.  It won't happen again.
