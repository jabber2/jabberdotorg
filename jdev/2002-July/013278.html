On Mon, 1 Jul 2002 13:44:43 +1000, Darren McVean wrote:
> From: Christof Meerwald [mailto:cmeerw at web.de]
>> I am looking for BETA testers for jabrss, a simple RSS headline notification
>> service. Just add jabrss at jabber.at to your Roster and have a look at
>> http://JabXPCOM.sunsite.dk/jabrss/ for how to subscribe to your favourite
>> RSS headlines.
> I would be interested in assisting you with beta testing. I've subscribed
> myself to your service and created a few rss files for it. Can you please
> send me through a link to get the files I need to install and run on my own
> internal system.

First you need to setup JabXPCOM (see http://JabXPCOM.sunsite.dk) which can
be a bit tricky. If you succeeded with setting up JabXPCOM, you just need to
get examples/jabrss.py and examples/parserss.py from the JabXPCOM CVS
repository (http://cvs.sunsite.dk/viewcvs.cgi/jabxpcom/) and run jabrss.py

If you have any questions about setting up JabXPCOM (and I know you will
have many), feel free to ask them on the JabXPCOM mailing list (see
http://sunsite.dk/mailman/listinfo/jabxpcom)
