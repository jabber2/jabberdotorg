It has been requested that the next part of this election process not be 
a live debate where you can ask questions in real time, but rather be on 
the mailing list.  So in the interest of keeping the piece and getting 
through this years elections here is how it will work.

Send any questions that you would like to see asked of the candidates to 
the members mailing list with the subject "Debate Question" by midnight 
Wednesday.  Someone, I'm hoping stpeter but I have not asked him yet, 
will take those questions and cull out the best six questions.  Then 
they will be presented to the candidates on Thursday and answered by Friday.

Again:

1) Send questions to members at jabber.org with the subject "Debate Question".
2) Must be in by Wednesday night midnight.
3) Someone will pull out six questions and post it to the mailing list 
on Thursday.
4) Candidates have 24 hours to answer.

Next year we may not even want to have this step, but let's see how it 
goes this year...
