XMLRPC over jabber is a draft standard that basically involves wrapping 
XMLRPC with a jabber packet (an <iq><query xmlns='jabber:iq:rpc'/></iq> 
to be precise) There is a jep on the subject located here 
http://www.jabber.org/jeps/jep-0009.html and a few different 
implementations available out at jabberstudio.org <plug 
type='shameless'> including my very own JabminRPC/C++ </plug>
