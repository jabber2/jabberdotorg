Sorry to tell you but JabberSMTP is not going to embed well into a 
mod_perl environment... it must maintain two socket connections(one for 
jabber one for SMTP). You are better off following the directions for 
setting up JabberSMTP as a stand alone component. And I am sure there is 
someone on this list that will be willing to contract support for you. 
If you have questions or suggestions for JSMTP talk to me... since i am 
the main developer on it these days :)
