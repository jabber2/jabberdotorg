Well, not necessarily.  If someone did make a Gaim transport, it would be able 
to run as many accounts as Gaim can.  The main reason the current transports 
only permit one registration per transport is due to the JID conversion 
convention they have chosen to use.

user at msn2.example.com, .... or move the 1 to basically anywhere else.  
Perhaps it could be encoded into the node even.

There was someone developing a Gaim transport a while back, but after a week 
or so nobody ever heard from them again.
