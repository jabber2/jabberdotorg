To get Jabber via anonymous CVS(version 1.10):
  [jeremie]$ export CVSROOT=:pserver:anoncvs at jabber.org:/cvs
  [jeremie]$ cvs login
  (Logging in to anoncvs at jabber.org)
  CVS password: *enter anything here*
  [jeremie]$ cvs checkout jabber
