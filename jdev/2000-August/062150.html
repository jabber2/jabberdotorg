Mark,

cvs -co -D "2 weeks ago" groupchat-transport

or grab it from http://download.jabber.org/dists/transports/groupchat-transport

I wouldn't use the groupchat-transport in CVS, even though I've updated it, it's running under the NEW groupchat protocol, from the JSD doc (http://core.jabber.org/summer.html (section 7.1 i think)) If you are a client author, and need to get your client up to speed with the new groupchat protocol, you can test your client against groups.jabber.org , which is groupchat-transport running under the new protocol.  

As soon as I update irc-transport, and we have a good client following of the new groupchat stuff, we can move everyone over.
