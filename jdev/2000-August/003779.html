After fiddling with various solutions offered, including the "--enable-dynamic=elf" solution...I found out that the problem is simply that after installing adns, I didn't do a "ldconfig" before installing libetherx...

To solve the problem, simply do a "ldconfig" first, then do a "make distclean" on libetherx and start the installation again.
