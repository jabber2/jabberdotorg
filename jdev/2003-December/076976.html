The Jabber Council has voted JEP-0047 (In-Band Bytestreams) to Draft.
This document defines a lightweight, fallback mechanism for file
transfer when SOCKS5 Bytestreams (JEP-0065) is not available. The IBB
protocol is also required for support of JEP-0096 (File Transfer).

http://www.jabber.org/jeps/jep-0047.html

Go forth and implement!
