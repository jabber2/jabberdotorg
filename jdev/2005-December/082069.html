Michael Finney wrote:
> In relation to groups that a contact can belong to, what is the valid group
> name syntax; just characters, numbers, and spaces?
> 
> Since I had the feeling that questions about valid names have been asked
> before, I've looked through the archive of this list and looked at the
> xmpp.org site. However, I do not see the answer. 

The schema says xs:string:

http://www.xmpp.org/schemas/roster.xsd

In XMPP that would mean really any UTF-8 character:

http://www.w3.org/TR/xmlschema-2/#string

Currently the length is not restricted. We may change that rfc3921bis 
after discussion on the xmppwg at jabber.org list.
