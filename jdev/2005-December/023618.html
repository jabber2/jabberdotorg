> The stream compression JEP seems useful in 3 situations:
>   1) You aren't encrypting at all
>   2) You're encrypting only with SASL
>   3) The TLS implementation can't do compression

Or:

     4) Your computing platform of choice doesn't support TLS (yet!) but 
you want to compress XMPP traffic

It is recommended to just set the compression bit in TLS but if you 
can't do that for whatever reason, JEP-0138 might help.
