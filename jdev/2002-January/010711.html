Hello, all. I've begun a polite, friendly petition to AOLTW about their
relation to popular Jabber servers. Many of my own users have already
started it off.

This welcome message says it all:
http://www.theoretic.com/jabber/discuss.cgi?read=3

Please take a moment to sign it, expressing your constructive thoughts
and requesting AOL to reconsider it's position towards Jabber. I would
love to see more suggestions to AOL about how they could still make
money and keep security by opening up and being interoperable with
Jabber.

Feel free to promote this petition how you want, as long as you don't
use spam or anything like it (I despise the use of spam). Again:
http://www.theoretic.com/jabber/discuss.cgi?read=3

Thanks, all.
