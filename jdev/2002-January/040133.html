On Mon, Jan 14, 2002 at 09:05:05AM -0600, John Reinke wrote:
> I was once told that groups could be created for distributed messaging,
> but I've not found a way to set them up, or a client that appears to
> support them. (I think I found something on the jabber.org web site a
> while ago, but can't find it back.) The closest thing for the clients is
> to send a list of users to someone else.

I think what you are looking for is called "Shared Groups" and is a
(little known/used?) feature provided by the mod_groups module. It
documented here:

    http://docs.jabber.org/server/html/groups.html

Jabber Inc./JIM seems to provide Shared Groups, too:

    http://support.jabber.com/jimhelpfiles/Shared_Groups.htm

I'm not sure though whether that's the same thing as mod_groups.

Hope this helps,
Jan
