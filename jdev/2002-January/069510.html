JabberTools 0.5.0 has been released and is available for download at 
http://sourceforge.net/project/showfiles.php?group_id=20248&release_id=70883 
This release is the first public release of the JabberTools tool suite
for Jabber end users and developers, though the Importer has been
previously released as a separate package.  Its tools aid in converting
to and using Jabber.  JabberTools currently has two parts -- the
Importer, which imports wide variety of contact lists, and the Account
Manager, which (as one might guess) helps manage your Jabber account.

The Importer supports the following list files:
     *  AOL IM (Windows and Linux)
     *  Gaim
     *  Gabber exported XML roster
     *  ICQ99b/2000 (Windows)
     *  Gerry's ICQ (Mac)
     *  mICQ
     *  Licq
     *  GnomeICU

The Account Manager supports:
     *  Create new accounts
     *  Remove your account
     *  Change your password

Both are available with commandline and web-based interfaces.

JabberTools' website is http://jabbertools.sourceforge.net/

Feedback is, of course, welcome.
