This month marks the beginning of the Year of the Ox.

The Berlin XMPP meetup will celebrate the new year with a talk about
OpenPGP for XMPP (OX) on:

  Wednesday, February 10, 2021, 18:00 CEST

We'll give a short introduction to the topic, followed by a panel
discussion with the following experts:

  DebXWoody (implementor of OX in Profanity)
  defanor (implementor of OX in rexmpp)
  Florian (co-author of the OX specifications)
  lovetox (implementor of OX for Gajim)
  Paul (implementor of OX in Smack)

The video conference URL will be announced in our public channel:

  xmpp:berlin-meetup at conference.conversations.im?join
