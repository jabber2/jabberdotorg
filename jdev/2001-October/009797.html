You need to use type="get".. here is what comes out of Winjab:

SENT: <iq type="get" id="wjSFields_wj_2" to="users.jabber.org"><query
xmlns="jabber:iq:search"></query></iq>
RECV: <iq from='users.jabber.org' id='wjSFields_wj_2'
to='pgmillard at jabber.org/workage' type='result'><query
xmlns='jabber:iq:search'><nick/><first/><email/><last/><instructions>Fill in
a field to search for any matching Jabber users.</instructions></query></iq>
