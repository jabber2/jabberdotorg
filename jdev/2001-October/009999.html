Hi David,

You need to define Jogger as a service in jabber.xml:

<service id="jogger.xxxx.xx">
	<accept>
		<ip>127.0.0.1</ip>
		<port>5226</port>
		<secret>jogger!</secret>
	</accept>
	<host>jogger.xxxx.xx</host>
</service>

Justin
