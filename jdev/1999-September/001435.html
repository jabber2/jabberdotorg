For all new Jabber-team members, please make a visit to docs.jabber.org.
There, all your questions shall be answered. (thanks e-t!)
</NOTICE>

Something like "how does Jabber seperate 'SuperJoe' on AIM and 'SuperJoe' on
Jabber?"

Answer: They both have different addresses. "SuperJoe" at AIM could be
"superjoe at aim.jabber.org" and "SuperJoe" on Jabber could be
"superjoe at jabber.org".
