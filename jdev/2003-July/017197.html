C: <?xml version='1.0'?>

           version='1.0'>
S: <?xml version='1.0'?>

           from='chat1.shakespeare.lit'
           id='id_123456789'

           xmlns:stream='http://etherx.jabber.org/streams'
           version='1.0'>

What happens?
