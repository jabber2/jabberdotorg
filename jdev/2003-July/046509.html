I released a new version of LLuna 2.4.3 with proxy support and chat composing notification and the usual small fixes. 
URL: http://www.lluna.de/ 
Jabberstudio CVS is up to date.

If you do not know LLuna: 
This is a cool application on the Jabber protocol. A Jabber client that lets you meet people on Web pages. Entirely based on Jabber, distributed presence system using jabber conf components, skinnable, bubble chat, avatars, embedded webcam, license LGPL. You will like it.
