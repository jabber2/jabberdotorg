On Wed, Mar 4, 2009 at 10:00 PM, Fabio Forno <fabio.forno at gmail.com> wrote:

> No, xep-138 and tls are mutually exclusive. When activating tls
> compression must be performed using tls compression.
> M-Link  should support it (at least the trunk version) and thanks to
> Dave Cridland we are going to test it.


XEP-0138 and TLS-compression SHOULD NOT be used at the same time (XEP-0138
says so).  XEP-0138 is allowed if TLS didn't negotiate compression.

We need a clear notion to show the difference between TLS (without
compression), TLS (with TLS compression), TLS (with XEP-0138 compression)
and TLS (with TLS and XEP-0138 compression - SHOULD NOT happen, but might)
