of web hosting services. I've been in contact with the folks at cPanel

their cPAddons system. Basically there is some packaging involved so
that your server software play along with cPanel. If you develop an XMPP
server and are interested in this opportunity, please ping me offlist
and I will hook you up with my contacts at cPanel.
