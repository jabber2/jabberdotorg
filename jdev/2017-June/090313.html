Hi All,

Our latest client for iOS has been offically released out of beta!

If you'd like to try our client, please visit http://goo.gl/J9xhGZ or
look for Tigase in the AppStore.

Tigase Messenger for iOS features:

Simple to Use Interface - Using XMPP has never been easier using a
familiar and easy to use interface.
Native Notification Support - Message notification using apple's
notification system.
Multiple Account Control - Control multiple accounts from a single
application and sign into all your XMPP accounts at once.
Local Archive - Localized chat history allows for quick browsing of
chats without needing to login.
MUC Supported - Support for joining and creating chatrooms for
multi-party collaboration.
Automated status management - No need to change status every time you
put your phone down.
Setup New Users in Seconds - Sign into a new account on your favorite or
your own XMPP/jabber server in seconds using in-band registration!
Bandwidth & Battery Saver - Software reduces communication needs while
application does not have focus or device is idle.
Advanced Security Supported - Keep your conversations secret with local
storage and data stream encryption using TLS and authentication with
SCRAM-SHA1.
Spam protection - When used in conjunction with Tigase XMPP Servers, you
get the added benefit of server side spam filtering and protection.

Our thanks to anybody who joined up on the beta, drawing will be held
soon and you will be notified by E-mail if you won.
