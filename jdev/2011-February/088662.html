An XEP which fits here is XEP-0144: Roster Item Exchange, which allows
suggesting roster entries. It also lets the initiator send their JID
first in a message, which avoids a roundtrip or two, and also allows
fallback to a text message. I have a vague recollection of some
clients actually implementing this.
