I am creating a Advocacy Manifesto 
[http://www.theoretic.com/?Jabber_Manifesto] as a promotional and 
introduction document for end users, outside developers, and executives 
or managers. It's meant to clear away many of the myths about Jabber 
going on in the "outside world", in an easy to understand and friendly 
manner. I am taking most of the material from my proposal to Kuro5hin to 
setup a Jabber server (which I will keep everyone updated on) 
[http://www.kuro5hin.org/story/2002/4/20/13027/2789].

Take a look at it, and feel free to add, edit, or otherwise improve the 
growing Advocacy Manifesto by making use of Theoretic's Wiki. Yes, my 
entire site is a Wiki (UseModWiki, to be exact [www.usemod.com]), so 
anyone can come in and edit the pages in real-time. To do so, follow the 
"Edit this Page" link near the top of the page, using the instructions 
found at "Formatting Rules".

Also, feel free to provide any feedback here if you don't like Wikis or 
feel uncomfortable with them. I'll make the changes you suggest.
