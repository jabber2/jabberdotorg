I've posted a news item on www.jabber.org about recent enhancements to the
Jabber protocol (Standards Track and Informational). In the future, I will
publish a news story on the site every time a protocol advances in the JEP
process to Draft or Final (Standards Track) or Active (Informational). If
you would like to be informed of such protocol changes, please subscribe
to the news at jabber.org mailing list (it is used only for announcements of
this kind, so it's very low volume) or the RSS feed, or just check the
website once in a while.

Thanks!
