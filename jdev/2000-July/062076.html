Subject Header: Announcing Jabber Bootcamp

Are you looking to implement Jabber or develop a Jabber-based application or
service? If so, then, you'll be happy to know about the Jabber Bootcamp,
designed to get you up and running with Jabber at rocket ship speeds. Sign
up today for this super-concentrated, one day, everything you need to know
about Jabber Bootcamp. This one day training will show you how to install,
configure, run and develop new applications for the Jabber platform. 

Jabber is the only Instant Messaging (IM) platform backed by an open-source
movement which gives every company control over their IM services through
Jabber's distributed client/server architecture. Take comfort that Jabber is
the most robust and sophisticated platform available through the use of XML
technology and a wide variety of Jabber development tools. 

The Training Team
Jabber Bootcamp is lead and managed by the Jabber.com Professional Services
group. The team is comprised of leading developers recruited from the Jabber
open-source development community and technical and project management
consultants from Jabber.com. Each member of the Jabber.com Bootcamp team has
extensive experience in setting up, configuring and developing for the
Jabber platform. 

Morning Session (8:30-12:00)
*	History of Jabber
*	Original Design Goals
*	Concepts of basic instant messaging
*	Jabber Distributed Client/Server Architectural Overview
*	Introduction to XML
*	Jabber XML primitives
*	Future Directions and Goals
*	Common Questions

Afternoon Session (1:00-5:30pm)
*	Setting up a typical Jabber server 
*	Setting up your own Jabber server 
*	Developing Clients for Jabber
*	Developing for the Jabber Server
*	Embedded Jabber in your own clients or products

Materials Provided
*	Jabber Bootcamp Manual

Course Capacity
*	12 People per Course

Course Date and Location
*	When: August 07, 2000 from 8:30am - 4:30pm 
*	Where: 1899 Wynkoop Suite 600  Denver, Colorado 80202
*	Jabber.com will provide lunch

Who should attend
*	Developers
*	System and Information Management Administrators

Cost
*	Jabber Overview (morning session only) $295 per person
*	Jabber Bootcamp (full day) $995 per person

How to Register

services at jabber.com
