ANNOUNCING THE FIRST EVER, JABBER.FEST 2000

Jabber.com is a sponsor of the O'Reilly Open Source Software Convention,
July 17-20, 2000 in Monterey, California. At the show, we'll be hosting the
first ever Jabber.fest. For more information on the O'Reilly convention,
check the convention web site (http://conferences.oreilly.com/oscon2000/).

Meet over 20 developers of the Jabber open-source project, including much of
the core development team. Free food and drinks! (limited to the first 200
people -- we can't feed the whole world...)

When: Wednesday, July 19th. 8pm-11pm.
Where: Doubletree Hotel, Monterey, CA in the Atrium Room.

Hope to see YOU there!!
