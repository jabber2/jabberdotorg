Along the same lines is the issue of resubscription.  What if the other party
doesn't want their info subscribed to from your other host, maybe they don't
have trust in it's admin, or they don't have a secure link to it.  Is it fair
for your contacts to be moved without question in that case?  I think not, so
the issue runs really deep.
