Ray -

You don't need to open/compile the XMLCom project... it was an encapsulation
of JUST the XML stuff which JabberCOM uses. If you want to compile just
JabberCOM, simply open that project and compile it.

NOTE: I haven't tried to compile JabberCOM on anything newer than Delphi 5
Prof, since thats what I used to develop it. I'm not sure if the string
libraries (HyperStr) have been ported to the newer compilers.
