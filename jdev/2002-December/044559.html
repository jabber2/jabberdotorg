We have an instant messaging system here at Monash called "goofey", that
was started in 1991. A reference:

  http://yoyo.cc.monash.edu.au/~tym/goofey.html

I use this every day for communication within our division, and although
I'm in the process of replacing it with a Jabber-based system, it still
gets a heavy workout.

Some samples:

% goofey -rw
      gub  IN (Dec 19 09:14)  0u I0:15
  faramir  IN (Dec 19 09:05)  0u I 44s L:28/223 
  hawkeye  IN (Dec 19 09:03)  0u I0:22
    stick  IN (Dec 19 08:42)  0u I0:11 L:Office 
    lanks  IN (Dec 19 08:18)  0u I0:07
      box  IN (Dec 11 18:11)  0u I????
      rmi OUT (Dec 11 15:38)  0u
   portal OUT (Apr  5 11:02)  0u


**** A message has arrived from pluto on Thu Dec 19 09:31! ****

portal goofed IN from translucent.its.monash.edu.au


**** A message has arrived from pluto on Thu Dec 19 09:32! ****
From: portal [To: box]
hi, how are you?


% goofey -s portal
Enter your message: (blank line or ^D to end, ~h for help)
> well, thanks for asking :)
>
Message sent to 'portal' at 1 location!


**** A message has arrived from pluto on Thu Dec 19 09:32! ****

portal goofed OUT from translucent.its.monash.edu.au
