Hello again,

I'd like to introduce you the remake of old JRuDevels' service
(http://habahaba.jrudevels.org) that built on the Jappix source code and
is being fork of it.

The service's aim is to make XMPP easier to a casual people. If you want
to connect with a man who haven't Jabber account at a time you can give
him your HabaHaba link and he will connect you just in two clicks.

You can see more info about it at JaWiki's page:
 http://jawiki.ru/Eng:Habahaba.im

Service itself located here: http://habahaba.im/

Trac: http://trac.jrudevels.org/habahaba/
