I have made an initial release of POE::Component::Jabber (for those of
you not familier with POE see http://poe.perl.org/). Its still at early
stages, but I've made it available for a bit of peer review and because
I wanted to get it out this side of christmas since I won't be around
much now till new year.

I'd welcome any comments on the design. I hope to extend it with alot
more classes to deal with higher level functions much as the P:C:J:Auth
module does now. If anyone wants to help out they should just dive in.

Thanks go to Dennis Taylor whos POE::Component::IRC this is almost
totally based on, also to DJ Adams for Jabber::Connection from which I
hacked the authentication and use of XML::Parser (I'm sure theres some
orginial code in somewhere).

The documentation sucks cause I don't have much time. Look at how
P:C:IRC works if you've not seen that before and perldoc
POE::Component::Jabber is the best I can do for now.

Grab it while its fresh from
ftp://ftp.morat.net/poe/POE-Component-Jabber-0.1.tar.gz
