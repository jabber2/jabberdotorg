If you need something right away, you can use MeatballWiki.

   http://www.usemod.com/cgi-bin/mb.pl

Proviso: you have to spend the half hour to figure out how 
to use the wiki first. Play nice, please.

You can usefully name the pages by their Jabber namespaces, 
e.g. http://www.usemod.com/cgi-bin/mb.pl?JabberXEvent for
jabber:x:event.

The standard way to comment on foreign pages is to put
a link at the top.

So, for example, on JabberXEvent, the page would be:

----8< [BEGIN EXAMPLE]-----------------------------------------
: http://docs.jabber.org/draft-proto/html/events.html

This is the jabber:x:event protocol. I really think it 
is a wonderful way to implement "Person is typing a reply"
functionality from MiribilisICQ. -- JaneRandomDeveloper
----8< [END EXAMPLE]-----------------------------------------
