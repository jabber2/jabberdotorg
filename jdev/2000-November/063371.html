> I'm behind a firewall, so what ports should I live open (besides 5222)?

You need port 5269 open as well, that is used for server-to-server
communications (5222 is used for client-to-server communications).

> Where can I find good transports to install?

Well.... All the transports are being upgraded to work with the 1.2
server, I don't think any of them are quite ready for prime time yet --
but see the recent JDEV archives
(http://mailman.jabber.org/pipermail/jdev/) for discussion of AIM-t,
temas has that one pretty far along. Or so he says. ;)
