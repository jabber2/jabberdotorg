Also keep in mind that although previous versions of ADNS supported it, 1.0
does not support looking up hosts within the /etc/hosts file. To use ADNS,
you will need to have components named with resolvable names. The other
option is to compile libetherx without ADNS (by commenting out the #define
HAVE_ADNS at the top of libetherx/include/config.h)
