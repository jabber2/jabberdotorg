Do remember though to obey the licenses of any code that you want to 
integrate into your product, i.e. you cant use GPL code in your product 
unless you are prepared to release your product under the GPL too, you 
certainly cant use any GPL code in any closed source products you might be 
developing, your best bet would be to use a more freely licensed code 
library for java.

have a look here for your various options
http://www.jabber.org/software/libraries.php
