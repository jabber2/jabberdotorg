Ok, figured out what the problem was with the autoconf setup. The following
lines need to be added to configure.in just before the AC_OUTPUT macro call:

AC_SUBST(SHELL)
AC_SUBST(LD)
AC_SUBST(LEX)
AC_SUBST(LIBTOOL)
AC_SUBST(LN_S)
AC_SUBST(NM)
