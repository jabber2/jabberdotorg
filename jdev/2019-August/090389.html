Hi Jon,

Maybe I should have added this in the announcement, but as FOSDEM is always at the ULB in Brussels, we co-locate the European XMPP Summit to be in the Brussels area, so that we can get more people in one location. At least since 2011 we've been kindly hosted by Cisco in their Diegem offices. We haven't finalized the venue yet for 2020, but wanted to announce the dates as soon as possible. Would love to see you there!

Doing an event in Nice is a great idea, though, so I'll raise that in our working group for Summits, Conferences And Meetups.

And thanks for your kind offer to (consider) sponsoring. Can you contact me privately to discuss in detail?
