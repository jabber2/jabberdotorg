Someone poked me offlist about these.

There are, in actuality, many protocol pieces in these documents, so it
may make sense to split them out. This will enable us to assign protocol
scores or ratings for XMPP in addition to the broader Jabber categories.
Thus I'm thinking it would make sense to specify things like:

tlsc2s
tlss2s
saslc2s
sasls2s
nameprep
nodeprep
resourceprep
resourcebind
dialback
stanzas
xmllang
imsession
presencesub
roster
privacyrules

I could keep the full xmppcore and xmppim features (easy shorthand if
you do it all), but also include the more granular features as well.

Thoughts?
