I have started a small, low-volume discussion list for folks who have
implemented or are just interested in BOSH (XEP-0124 and XEP-0206):

http://mail.jabber.org/mailman/listinfo/bosh

The list will be used to coordinate interop testing, share
implementation experience, provide spec feedback, etc.

BTW, the list of BOSH clients and servers is growing. Tomas Karasek (who
is working on BOSH support in Gajim for the Google Summer of Code) made
a pretty comprehensive list today at wiki.jabber.org:

http://wiki.jabber.org/index.php/Bidirectional-streams_Over_Synchronous_HTTP_(BOSH)_(XEP-0124)
