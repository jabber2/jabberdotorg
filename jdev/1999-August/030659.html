OPEN SOURCE FOR MSN MESSENGER SERVICE
http://www.microsoft.com/presspass/features/1999/08-18messenger.htm
Microsoft is taking steps to ensure that will all instant messaging users,
no matter which service they use, can communicate quickly and seamlessly
across the Internet. Microsoft's solution is to publish the protocol for its
MSN(tm) Messenger Service, a move that reinforces its commitment to
fostering free and open communication.
