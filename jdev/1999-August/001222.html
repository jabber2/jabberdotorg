On Tue, 10 Aug 1999, Scott Robinson wrote:

> Are we able to have more than one <action> tag inside a filter?
> Clarification wanted. ;)

I was about to say no, but why the *beep* not? Of course you can have
several action tags! Like this:

<filter from=".*">
  <action do="accept">
  </action>
  <action do="accept">
    <to>secretary</to>
  </action>
</filter>
