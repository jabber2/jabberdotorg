We really really shouldn't develop our own language. That's not the goal of
this project. Instead, I'd recommend we use ECMAScript/Javascript since it's
fairly close to what we need (window oriented stuff), and simply extend it
with what we need. Kind of like NN5 (anybody played with a beta build?

preference settings, which is what we're basicaly interested in);

well. Use PNG for images (it's an excellent format, but can use all the help
it can get). And sound in au.
