Does it have to be in the presence info? I noticed the (unused?) XML-vCard
spec has fields for such things as a users GPS location - which is way cool,
but I don't think has ever been used.

I would love to have my client display a small map of the world with a
highlight where the user you are chatting with is (great for the geography
impaired like me!).

Perhaps your time would be better spent trying to get a consensus on a
decent implementation of the vCard or similar that all clients can use, and
just pull the info that they require, rather than loading up the presence
tags.
