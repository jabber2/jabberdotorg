Hi!

I have inserted a small patch in the yahoo-transport. It's the change
Philip S. Tellis made to the yahoo module of Everybuddy. It's and
unknwon header field in the yahoo protocol.

As my yahoo account works well I can't check if this really solves the
problem. Also Philip posted yesterday on the Everybuddy mailinglist that
his Everybuddy patch discontinued to work in India.
It seems Yahoo! is checking the protocol change there first. A new linux
version of the original Yahoo! client is also only available on the
Indian website of Yahoo! yet.

The new snapshot of yahoo-t is available at
http://amessage.de/patches/yahoo-transport-0.8.4.6.tar.bz2
