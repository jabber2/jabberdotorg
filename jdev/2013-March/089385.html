Hi,

XEP-0198[1] (Stream Management) mentions TLS session resumption in
section 5:

> 5. Client sends STARTTLS request.
> 6. Server informs client to proceed with the TLS negotiation.
> 7. The parties complete a TLS handshake. (Note: When performing
>    session resumption and also utilizing TLS, it is RECOMMENDED to
>    take advantage of TLS session resumption to further optimize the
>    resumption of the XML stream.)


   [1]: http://xmpp.org/extensions/xep-0198.html
