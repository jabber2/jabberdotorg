Hi.  Jer and I have put together a Jabber Open Source Project survey. 
We're trying to find out how everyone feels about the project in general
not the code in particular.  The idea is to figure out what we can do
both separately and individually to make Jabber a better open source
project for everyone.  The survey is being hosted at:

  http://hendrix.jabber.com/cgi-bin/open/survey.pl

it should take only a couple of minutes to go through and will hopefully
be worth your while by helping us make the project better.  The results
will be made available for public review so make it look good!  And,
hey, please be gentle with an old Perl hack.  Don't cut and paste the
site HTML into the text boxes.  B)

If you have any questions or concerns, just let me know.  Thanks!
