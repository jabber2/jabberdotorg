Guys.. the cause is most likely due to socket limits on the s2s jabberd running on
kelvin.  *many* *many* servers are making connections to kelvin due to update.jabber.org
and users.jabber.org being hit by just about every client that logs into jabber.
This is why it seems to work intermittently.. cause sometimes there are sockets free,
and sometimes there are not... the timeouts are set very low on these sockets, and
free up as quickly as possible to make room for more..

unfortunitly, this will be like this until polld is merged into the MIO backend, to
allow for much greater connections than the select backend that MIO is currently using.

Sorry for the annoyance, but at least you know what the deal is...

Keith Minkler
