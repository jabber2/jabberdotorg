Where is your jabber2 folder located? if it is in /src/jabber2 for example,
you need to pass configure ./configure --with-jabberd=/src/jabber2 it is looking
for both the jabberd.h header, and the lib.h header, and expecting to find
them in [/path]/jabberd and [/path]/jabberd/lib
