On Mon, Jun 06, 2005 at 03:33:26PM -0700, Greg Ferguson wrote:
> On your page that describes possible projects you list several
> server/client projects.  If I wanted to work specifically with one of
> these projects, like Jive, how would it work?  Who should I confer with
> when planning my proposal?

The Jabber Software Foundation is really the "umbrella" organization 
for the Summer of Code projects -- if you are interested in working 
on a specific codebase (e.g., ejabberd, Jive Messenger, Exodus, Psi)
then I think it makes sense to seek out one of the core developers on
that project. If you don't know whom to talk with, feel free to ping
me and I'll connect you to the right people.
