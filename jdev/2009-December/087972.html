The CC licenses all assert copyright, whereas PD is a waiver of  
copyright.

As you say, in Europe, there's actually no such thing as Public  
Domain, as such, so the actual legal standing of the copyright in  
works authored by European member state nationals claimed to be  
public domain is vaguely hazy, but I suspect any judge would take it  
to be a permissive license in the spirit to which it's intended.
