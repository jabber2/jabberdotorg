On Fri, May 07, 2004 at 02:22:31AM +0200, Maciej Dziardziel wrote:
> Is presence packet one of those "special"? As i understand, when components
> binds to domain (sending only <bind> tag) other components automaticly
> receive (un<routed>) <presence> packet, but what about sending <presence>
> (with status)?

If you receive a raw <presence/> packet from the router, then you know
its a domain advertisement. If you receive <route><presence/></route>,
then you know its from a component. Its meaning in that case entirely
depends on what the sending component is.
