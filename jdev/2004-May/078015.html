If you have added the sending domain of this list to a "known hosts"
lists, you may have recently received an error message like this:

******

May 25 00:03 reject_warning: RCPT from unknown[208.245.212.109]: 450
Client host
+rejected: cannot find your hostname, [208.245.212.109];
+from=<jadmin-bounces at jabber.org> to=<you at example.com> proto=ESMTP
+helo=<hades.jabber.org>

******

You must update your known hosts list with the "hades.jabber.org"
domain. Previously, the sending domain was a long hostname like
"hades.all-your-im-are-belong-to-us.jabber.org", which was a little 
joke from Jer, but unfortunately that hostname did not resolve in
DNS. The actual machine name is "hades" and the sending domain of
this list and all other jabber.org lists is "hades.jabber.org".

Sorry for the lack of notice.
