The Jabber Council has voted to advance JEP-0033 (Extended Stanza
Addressing) to a status of Draft within the Jabber Software Foundation's
standards process. This JEP defines a protocol that enables entities to
include RFC822-style address headers for XMPP stanzas in order to
specify multiple recipients or sub-addresses. Implementations are now
encouraged.

http://www.jabber.org/jeps/jep-0033.html
