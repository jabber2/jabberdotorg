On 3/8/06, Robert B Quattlebaum, Jr. <darco at deepdarc.com> wrote:
> My friend who is using trillian says that the trillian log is complaining
> that <cli:message ...> isn't a valid top level stanza and drops it. Looks
> like a namespace issue of some sorts? Pandion doesn't seem to work too well
> either...

This problem was caused by some of the namespace patches used to make
jabberd2 work with the Google Talk Service. The patches are discussed
here: http://j2.openaether.org/bugzilla/show_bug.cgi?id=159 . Some of
these patches add a namespace declaration to the stanza element.  The
Google Talk Service passed these declarations on to clients, possibly
in a modified form.

The Google Talk Service was recently updated to filter out these
namespace problems
