Hello all,

We're pleased to announce the release of Spark Fastpath -- a commercial
extension to Wildfire and Spark that provides queuing and routing of IM
conversations. The two typical use cases:

 * Deploy the included web client to your website so that customers can
chat with you.
 * Setup workgroups for departments inside your organization such as
"HR" or "IT Helpdesk" to make it easier to for people to find the best
person to answer their questions.

More information and evaluation downloads at:

http://www.jivesoftware.com/products/fastpath/ 

Or, read more about the release:

http://www.instantmessagingplanet.com/enterprise/article.php/3591571


Normally, I only post announcements for major releases of our Open
Source or free software to Jdev. However, I thought the Fastpath release
warranted a posting since it's an implementation of JEP-0142
(Workgroups). Those of you following that JEP may remember our earlier
product iteration called Jive Live Assistant (hence the 3.0 version
number for Fastpath). I also hoped people might be interested in the
commercial work we're doing on top of our Open Source base. :)

Regards,
Matt
