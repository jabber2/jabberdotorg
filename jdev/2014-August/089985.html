Hi everyone,

As you may have read there's a XMPP summit taking place in Berlin, Germany
on the 10th September 2014.

Sorry for the late notice, but with Realtime conf coming to a close, no
strong call for a NA summit, and because several of us will be in Berlin
around this time it seemed sensible to organise something rather than have
nothing.

If you could please mark your attendance on the wiki page here
http://wiki.xmpp.org/web/Summit_16 so we can organise catering (provided we
have sponsorship to cover) and the correct sized room. If there are items
you would like to discuss please add them there too.

I will try and organise video conferencing facilities so those not
attending will be able to actively participate.

Everyone is welcome, however please see below for a potentially more
suitable event.

The other idea is to have a hackathon on the Thursday (or continue summit
discussions) where we can work on the XSF website, discuss XEPs, and do
some hacking on ideas. I'm thinking inviting the wider community to this
and potentially have barcamp/introductory talks - and calling it xmpp.js
just because its JSFest. I'm speaking at a decentralize.js event on the
Tuesday so can advertise and we will hopefully pull in some of that
community too.

Cheers, Lloyd.
