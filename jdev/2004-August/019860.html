	At xmpp.pubsub.com, our Jabber/XMPP server supports PubSub
(JEP-0060). However, that is all it supports. You can only use PubSub -- not
any of the other Jabber/XMPP protocols except those like disco, forms, SHIM,
etc. that are required by JEP-0060. Yes, I realize that probably sounds a
little weird.
