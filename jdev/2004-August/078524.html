According to spec[1], the <x xmlns='jabber:x:event'/> is supposed to 
contain the <id/>.  This would appear to be a bug with tkabber, not yahoo-t.


-  LW

[1] JEP-0022: Message Events <http://www.jabber.org/jeps/jep-0022.html>
