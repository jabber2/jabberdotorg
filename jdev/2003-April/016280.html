Hello Ronan,

  MU-Conference fully implements pretty much everything that is outlined
in the JEP document. The only exception is that you are not currently
able to register with a room. I am not implementing this until I can
work out what is/can be gained by doing this, and making sure it is
handled sanely. That is the only thing, and even that was only a
suggestion in the JEP.

  Regarding clients that support the full range of functionality, I know
that Tkabber is almost complete. Exodus also has a wide range of
functionality, however I haven't found a way yet of listing what a users
role and affiliation is in a room. I know there are other clients that
support MUC, however I haven't been able to personally try them yet.

Regards,

  David
