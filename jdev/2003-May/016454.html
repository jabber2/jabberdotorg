Jabber Developers;

Please don't lose patience.

The Jabber Gateway Framework is a real, working piece of code. It is very complete and stable. Implementing server extensions using the framework simplifies the development drastically. It has been through 2 major restructurings having been in development for a little over a year now. I am looking to release it into the open source ASAP. If all things go well it will be released in late May or early June.

I am very happy to see that there is interest in this work. Please stay tuned for an announcement of it's release. I don't think that you will be disappointed.

Thanks Again,
Joe.
