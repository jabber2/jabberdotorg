Folks,

As you know, the IESG has approved our proposal to move forward with the
XMPP Working Group at the IETF. Now is the time for us to get busy! If
you are at all interested in helping us to complete the RFC revisions,
define improved methods of end-to-end encryption, and work on other core
topics for XMPP, please subscribe to the xmpp at ietf.org list and weigh in
(even just to vote +1 on accepting various WG items, to provide some of
your implementation and deployment feedback there, etc.).

Thanks!
