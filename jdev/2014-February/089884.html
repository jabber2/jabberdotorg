Hi all,

As you may/may not know the next XMPP UK meetup is being held on the 4th
March in London to coincide with the IETF meeting.

Details here:

   - http://lanyrd.com/2014/xmppuk/, or here,
   - http://www.meetup.com/XMPP-UK-Meetup/events/166277882/

We aim to put hold roughly three talks/demonstrations during the evening
and, to that end, we're asking for volunteers from the community to
present/demo at the event.

The topic can be on anything XMPP related, even as far as holding a
discussion around a particular topic of interest.

If you are interested in any of the above please get in touch with myself
either on this email address or lloyd.watkin at surevine.com. I/we'd also
appreciate you sharing the event links via social media to help build
interest.

Surevine will be supplying pizza and beer on the night, and also thanks to
Mozilla for being our hosts for the evening.

Cheers, Lloyd.
