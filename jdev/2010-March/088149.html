The XSF has been accepted this year to participate in the 2010 Google
Summer of Code - woo!

But... that means now we need ideas and mentors :)

If you are a member of the XSF and have any ideas for projects that
you would think would make a great GSoC project, please add them to
the Project Ideas wiki page.

http://wiki.xmpp.org/web/Summer_of_Code_2010_Project_Ideas

This will give potential students an idea on what projects to consider
and also help us gauge what mentors we need to coodinate.

Note that I will be editing the project list for grammar, spelling and
other nit-picking reasons and also to remove the crazy ideas or the
ideas that just are not in the scope of a GSoC project.

thanks!
