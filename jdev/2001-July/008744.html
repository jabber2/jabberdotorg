One thing I'd like to throw out here is the possibility of using a common
design with the Jabelin folks. I think we may be best served by
participating in the design process with the Jabelin folks, which I believe
is just starting this process. We may want to consider having a 'subsig'
under Jabelin, using an 'org.jabber' namespace.

I think it's great that Al opened up his source, and I'm not trying to
discourage its use, but we should really think about some of the broader
organizational and design issues before we go much further.
