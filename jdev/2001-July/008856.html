Jabber at the O'Reilly Open Source Convention

The following was posted by stpeter at jabber.org via the Jabber DevZone web site (http://dev.jabber.org/):



The Jabber Inc. booth (manned by jer, stpeter, Michael Bauer, and
Griffin Caruolo) will probably be the center of Jabber discussion
throughout the week at the O'Reilly Open Source Convention. In
addition, here's a cheat sheet covering the chronology of
Jabber-related events:



1. jer and stpeter will run a Birds of a Feather session on Tuesday
evening ( 6:30 - 7:30, Harbor Island II, 
http://conferences.oreillynet.com/cs/os2001/pub/w/os2001/bofs.html )



2. DJ Adams and Piers Harding will speak on Jabber and Perl on
Wednesday morning ( 10:45 - 11:30, Grande Ballroom B,
http://conferences.oreillynet.com/cs/os2001/view/e_sess/1438 )



3. Webb Interactive CEO Perry Evans will discuss Jabber as an
open-source IM platform on Wednesday afternoon ( 2:45 - 3:15, Catalina
Ballroom, http://conferences.oreillynet.com/cs/os2001/view/e_sess/1760
)



4. Eric Murphy will talk about his work on Jabberzilla on Thursday
afternoon ( 4:30 - 5:15, Harbor Island III,
http://conferences.oreillynet.com/cs/os2001/view/e_sess/1280 )



5. Carlos de la Guardia will present "Jabber as a Platform for
Specialized Messaging Services" on Friday ( 2:30 - 3:15, Point Loma A,
http://conferences.oreillynet.com/cs/os2001/view/e_sess/1384 )



6. Michael Bauer of Jabber Inc. and the Jabber Foundation will round
out this unofficial "Jabber track" with a talk on Friday afternoon (
3:45 - 4:30, Bel Aire South,
http://conferences.oreillynet.com/cs/os2001/view/e_sess/1913 )



We hope to see you there, and will post a full report after the
conference. And don't forget, only one more month until JabberCon! 
--stpeter
