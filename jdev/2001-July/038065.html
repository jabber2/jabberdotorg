All,

I've taken a break from the Java Jabber server to knock together a quick
system which I would appreciate some feedback on.

The system informs you via Jabber when new mail is going to your mailbox.
This means you don't have to have your Email client constantly refreshing or
keep hitting reload on your webmail interface.

It's only available on mail going to the personalbuddy.com domain (how to
get an account is at http://www.personalbuddy.com, and all Emails are
forwarded onto your normal mailbox). It's not a publicly available feature,
so once you have an account drop me a mail and I'll enable it.

Enjoy,
