Just to let the developer community know:
We have ported the Jabberoo library, along with libSigC++, to Windows CE.
The source is freely downloadable from http://www.movsoftware.com/devres.htm
and will work on all Windows CE 3.0+ devices.

I will be extending the library to include additional functionality and will
be releasing the updates at the same download link. If you have any
comments/questions please let me know.
