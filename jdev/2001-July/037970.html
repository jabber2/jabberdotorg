I believe this means you need to actually specify the ip's that these 
ports are listening to. In the c2s and s2s sections of the jabber.xml 
configuration file, there should be an <ip/> tag - change this to 
something of the form <ip>127.0.0.1</ip>. In the past, that has solved 
this problem on non-Linux platforms.
