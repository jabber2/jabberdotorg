Well, as with any service : they visit our website (they can access only
'public' spaces, including a signup page) and then they signup. Once signed
up, they can access their privatate and they get a login/password for the
jabber server!
The goal here is to do the same with XMPP : they connect "anonymously" to
the component : they request a login and password and then they can access
their private information and use the jabber server.
