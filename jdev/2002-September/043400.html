Welcome to the jdev at jabber.org mailing list! This list has a large
base of subscribers, so please take extra caution when sending to the
list.  Please do not post offtopic messages or spam. Questions related
to administration of Jabber servers should be sent to the JADMIN list,
not the JDEV list. You can sign up for JADMIN at
http://mailman.jabber.org/listinfo/jadmin/

The issues on jdev at jabber.org are often technical, please make sure
you are very familiar with Jabber and have read all of the developer
docs at http://docs.jabber.org/ and all of this list's archives.


To post to this list, send your email to:

  jdev at jabber.org

General information about the mailing list is at:

  http://mailman.jabber.org/listinfo/jdev

If you ever want to unsubscribe or change your options (eg, switch to
or from digest mode, change your password, etc.), visit your
subscription page at:

  http://mailman.jabber.org/options/jdev/madhu%40wizit.com

You can also make such adjustments via email by sending a message to:

  jdev-request at jabber.org

with the word `help' in the subject or body (don't include the
quotes), and you will get back a message with instructions.

You must know your password to change your options (including changing
the password, itself) or to unsubscribe.  It is:

  khammam1

If you forget your password, don't worry, you will receive a monthly
reminder telling you what all your jabber.org mailing list passwords
are, and how to unsubscribe or change your options.  There is also a
button on your options page that will email your current password to
you.

You may also have your password mailed to you automatically off of the
Web page noted above.
