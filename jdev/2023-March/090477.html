I have setup the membership application Wiki page for the XSF membership 
application period Q2 2023.

Applications are encouraged from developers, users and others who are 
actively involved in the Jabber/XMPP community.
To apply, create a page about yourself on the Wiki:
https://wiki.xmpp.org/web/Membership_Applications_Q2_2023

If you don't have a wiki account, send your full name, preferred 
nickname and email address to me or one of the other Sysops:
https://wiki.xmpp.org/web/Sysops

When you have questions related to the application process then don't 
hesitate to contact me directly.

Apply now!!!
