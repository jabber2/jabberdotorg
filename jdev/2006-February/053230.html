This is a first  release of jabber2sms transport aspsms-t.

* Global Two-Way SMS (Receiving of sms messages directly to the jabber client)
* Delivery notification for each sms via jabber
* Alphanumeric originator (max 11 characters) of every SMS.
* Over 400 mobile networks in 200 countries
* Recharge SMS credits via Internet

More information at 
* http://www.micressor.ch/content/projects/aspsms-t
* http://www.aspsms.com/solutions/3rd-party/jabbertosms/
* http://jabberstudio.org/projects/aspsms-t/news/view.php?id=662
* http://svn.micressor.ch/public/aspsms-t/trunk
