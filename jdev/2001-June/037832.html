One of the things that's been suggested for encouraging Jabber developers is
the publication of a Jabber Roadmap.  This would be something similar to the
Mozilla roadmap (http://www.mozilla.org/roadmap.html) and would help show
where Jabber is going and where there are opportunities to help.  I've put
together another little survey at
http://support.jabber.com/cgi-bin/foundation/roadmap/survey.pl to help
collect your thoughts on where Jabber could go.  

As you'll see in the preamble to the survey, anything and everything goes.
Not only suggestions for core software development, but documentation, and
even site development suggestions are welcome.  Some of this information is
already available in other places throughout the Jabber universe but
collecting it all in one place and publishing it prominently will help
people understand where we're going and what they can do to help at a
glance.  Please take a moment and contribute your thoughts.  If you have any
questions, let me know!  Thanks.
