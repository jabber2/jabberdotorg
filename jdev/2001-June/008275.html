The XML Spec (http://www.w3.org/TR/REC-xml) yields the following under 
2.3 Common Syntactic Constructs (specifically, the "literals" subsection):

AttValue    ::=    '"' ([^<&"] | Reference)* '"' |  "'" ([^<&'] | 
Reference)* "'"

Notice that " and ' are both acceptable.
