I believe that port 5222 is set up for client to server communication, 
so connecting to that port with a component namespace will be invalid.  
You will need to put something like:

 <service id="myservicelinker">
   <host>myservice.localhost</host>
   <accept>
     <ip>127.0.0.1</ip> <!--Or whatever the IP of the component is-->
     <port>5233</port>
     <secret>mysecret</secret>
     <timeout>30</timeout>
   </accept>
 </service>

In your jabber.xml file.  If you want the service to be browsable you 
will also need to add something to the browse section of your jabber.xml

file.
