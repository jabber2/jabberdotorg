> I'm not sure I see how this would increase it so much?  You would have
> to drastically alter the roster protocol so that groups where stored
> external of items so that you had one master list.  Then you'd have to
> introduce refs (or keep the names, but what'st he point then?) back to
> the master list.  It's fairly trivial to pull in the group names as you
> process the roster and keep theme sorted.

In theory, if you really wanted this to persist across clients, you could
just dump the ordering into a known namespace of jabber:iq:private and
trust to clients to support it.  I happen to think this is sort of
overkill, though, because most clients support some method of ordering
things the way you want them on your local side.
