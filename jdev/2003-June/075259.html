Gaim isn't skinnable, it's themeable.  All of the gaim developers agree
that we have no desire to implement skinning in the current UI.  That
said, Gaim will very soon be core/ui split, and then an appropriately
skilled person can stick whatever user interface they want on it, and
have most of the hard work done for them.  Until then, everyone gets to
enjoy plain old GTK+ themes.

On a side note, I don't want to pick on you Mattias, you're just one of
many:

I know it seems like I'm nit-picking, but please make a concious effort
to say Gaim or gaim, and not GAIM or gAIM.  The difference is the first
two cause us no legal trouble, and the second two have legal issues
associated with them.
