Yeap this kind of stuff!

What I have wanted since I first saw SMS years back is a MUC room that
dynamically sends to best try - online or else SMS to the phone.

 E.g. "Tim's Friday Nite pub crawl" room where I can select who is in for
that particular night. I send one message and it is distributed to the
party. People can then SMS once only to send to all the people and so manage
the status of the event, change of venue or time etc.

Once we get 3G or 4G this kind of private room could be hot with the concept
of multi user voice chat/voice bites as well as texting.

The issue of getting transports firm is a given. Blocking? Well this might
therefore suggest a personal server to provide the transports after
all...but with account details held centrally to enable multipoint access
with one logon.
