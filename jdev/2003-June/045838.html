Francisco Goncalves at PT Inovacao has informed me of a research project
being conducted by Eurescom to analyze the future potential of both the
fixed and wireless/mobile instant messaging markets, with a special
focus on implications for telecommunications companies. Information
about the study is available here:

http://www.eurescom.de/public/projects/P1300-series/P1345/default.asp

Francisco is helping out with this project and would like to gather 
information from members of the Jabber community regarding companies
that are involved in the IM marketplace, future trends, etc.. If you 
are interested in assisting Francisco, please contact him via email 
or Jabber:

email: est-f-goncalves at ptinovacao.pt
JID: chico at uniboxow.ptinovacao.pt
