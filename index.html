Jabber.org is the original messaging service based on XMPP and has been continuously offered for free since 1999.

15th January 2023 - service status update: Our migration went according to plan, and the service is now running again! Note that it may take a while before the service is fully accessible for some people while DNS updates take effect.

Due to time constraints, our priority has been to migrate active accounts. If you had an inactive account at jabber.org, you may receive a message that your account is disabled when you attempt to log in. Please try again in a few days, and have patience while we migrate the remaining accounts to the new system.

To log in, use one of the XMPP-compatible messaging apps listed at <https://xmpp.org/xmpp-software/clients/>.

This service is run by volunteers, with hosting by USSHC and open-source software from the Prosody team.

If you are having trouble logging in, please check the service notices.

Please read our service policy.
